About Me

I am a Professor of Management Science and Operations at London Business School. My research focuses on healthcare management, operations management, and the application of data science to improve organizational performance. I hold a PhD in Management Science from the Judge Business School, University of Cambridge, an MPhil in Finance (Financial Engineering) from the University of Cambridge, and a BA in Physics from the University of Cambridge.
